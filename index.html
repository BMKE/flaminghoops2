About Us

Who we are and how we coach

Khai, Flaming Hoops Head Coach

Flaming Hoops basketball coaching specialises in individual training, small group sessions, team training/games and holidays camps. We cater for all ages and abilities whether the player is striving to make a representative or school team or develop their skills and teamwork. We focus on character and skill development, learning the fundamentals of the game as well as creating an enjoyable and supportive environment for all our players.
